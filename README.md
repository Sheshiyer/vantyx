## How it works

| | | |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **1 · Capture** | **2 · Publish** | **3 · Explore** |
| Shoot equirectangular 360°s for each floor, view direction, and time of day. | Your team uploads and publishes in the browser — atomic and non-destructive, so the live tour never goes down. | Buyers step onto any floor, at any hour, from any device — just a link. |

## Who it's for

**View-led property developers** and their **sales & marketing teams** — anyone selling on outlook and lifestyle, who needs the pitch to stay honest as the building changes.

- A premium, **branded tour per project**, on your own subdomain.
- **Always-current views** — re-capture and publish as the floors rise.
- **Self-serve** by non-technical staff — no developer in the loop.
- **Built on Cloudflare's global edge** — fast everywhere, updates without downtime.
